Abouts us

Qiraad Inc. is a Wyoming, US based corporation that develops applications for capital markets. We started operations in 2019 with the vision to build an ecosystem that will unify all investing resources into a single platform that we named “tahlil”, the Arabic word for analysis. A universal problem for investors has always been the thinking required for investing decisions. As each investor is unique, we believe that each investor deserves the flexibility to codify their unique edge and invest on their own terms. With maximum automation as our aim, we have set out to build Middle East’s premium data & analytics solution:
tahlil.finance is an application developed from the scratch using latest technologies to offer unparalleled investing experience with 300+ trading indicators, 10,000+ data points per instrument, complete order functionality, elegant & intuitive trading platform, portfolio & risk analytics, financial journalism and social media analytics.

Built from ground up for all investor types.
